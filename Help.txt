To use menus:-

1, Go to the "Data" menu and click on "Load" submenu.

2, Click on the menu/submenu you want to use.

3, Follow instructions under the code if present.

4,  Go to the "Data" menu again and click on "List" to see the result.

General Instruction:-

1, Click on one of the movie catagory buttons to view list of movies.

2, Click on the button of a a movie to view more about it.

3, Click on "RENT" if you want to rent the movie.

4, Find your receipt under the code.

5, Use the "Back" menu if you want to go back at any moment.

6, Use the "Exit" menu if you want to exit at any moment.
